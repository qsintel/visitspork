Your Story

You arrive in Spork with nothing but hope and determination. The city is split down the middle - left lies the West, where money talks and corporate towers scrape the sky. Right lies the East, where the forgotten struggle to survive and dream of revolution.

Unlike the border guards who decide your fate, you are the immigrant. You are the one being judged, inspected, and processed. Papers, Please showed you the other side of the checkpoint - now you're the one standing in line, hoping your documents are in order, hoping you'll be allowed to pass.

But getting in is just the beginning. In Spork, survival means choosing a side. Will you climb the corporate ladder in the West, trading your soul for financial security? Or will you join the resistance in the East, building reputation and fighting for a better world?

The choice is yours. But remember - in Spork, every choice has consequences, and neutrality is not an option. Choose wisely, immigrant. Your life depends on it.
